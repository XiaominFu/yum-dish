Welcome!

This website provides unique insights into Yelp reviews of US restaurants. It reveals Yelp user clustering patterns and predict restaurant ratings using machine learning techniques. It also creates informative visualizations of Yelp reviews for particular restaurants.

Bad restaurants or critical users?

 

Insights

Restaurants with low ratings are disproportionately reviewed by critical users.
Critical users either:
(1) tend to give lower ratings compared to average users (for the same service), or
(2) are more likely to write reviews when encountering bad experiences.
