Part 1. Bad restaurants or critical users? An analysis of user clusters in Yelp reviews

Number of reviews: 1,244,839
Number of restaurants: 19,737 (in 6 US cities)
Number of users: 325,714 (with at least 2 reviews)
Data size: 1.04 GB
Takeaway: Restaurants with low ratings are disproportionately reviewed by critical users.

Critical users either:
(1) tend to give lower ratings compared with average users (for the same service), or
(2) are more likely to write reviews when encountering bad experiences.
